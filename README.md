# AP Group of Industries - E-Commerce Platform

Online marketplace for premium RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) products and construction materials.

## Products
- **RCC Compound Walls** - 50+ decorative styles (Lattice, Carved, Colored, Brick-finish)
- **Precast Boundary Walls** - Fast installation for farms, industrial & residential areas
- **RCC Benches & Garden Sets** - Park benches, armrest benches, round table sets
- **Other RCC Products** - Custom solutions available

## Features
- Product catalog with specifications & pricing
- Product gallery with high-quality images
- Shopping cart & secure checkout
- Order management & tracking
- WhatsApp integration for quick inquiries
