Our Process

Manufacturing Process

From raw materials to finished precast product — precision at every step

Factory Floor

Precision Manufacturing at Patiala

Our Patiala factory produces every precast RCC product in-house — from raw material mixing to final finishing. Steel precision moulds ensure consistent dimensions batch after batch.

High-carbon steel and 4mm HT wire reinforcement is embedded in every structural product before concrete is poured, vibrated, and cured for 7–14 days to full design strength.

Step by Step

Our 7-Step Process

01

Raw Material Procurement

High-grade white stone dust, river sand, graded aggregate, high-carbon steel wire, and 4mm HT wire — each batch inspected before production begins.

02

Steel Mould Preparation

Precision steel moulds cleaned, inspected, and release-agent treated. Dimensions verified to specification — 2100×300×50mm planks, 2400×150×150mm columns.

03

Steel Reinforcement Placement

High-carbon steel and 4mm HT wire is cut, bent, and placed per design specs inside the mould before concrete is poured.

04

Concrete Batching & Pouring

Precisely batched concrete mix poured into moulds and vibro-compacted to eliminate air voids — ensuring maximum density and strength.

05

Surface Finishing

Decorative textures, carvings, lattice cutouts, and colour finishes applied during the fresh-concrete stage using pattern moulds.

06

Curing (7–14 Days)

Demoulded elements water-cured under gunny bags or in water ponds for 7–14 days to achieve full design strength.

07

Inspection & Dispatch

Each element inspected for cracks, defects, and dimensional accuracy before careful loading and dispatch to site.
